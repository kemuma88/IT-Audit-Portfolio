About This Portfolio
This repository documents my hands-on IT Audit and GRC training, built week by week alongside a structured IT Audit bootcamp. Each lab is grounded in real audit frameworks (COSO, SOX, PCI DSS, ISO 27001) and follows industry-standard workpaper format used by IT auditors in public accounting firms, internal audit departments, and consulting engagements.
My background in Fortune 500 program and project management — spanning CenterPoint Energy, Labcorp, Stellantis, Ford, and Apple — gives me a strong operational foundation for understanding the business context behind every control I audit.
